# Tableau-Based Infrastructure Assessment & Exploratory Data Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing rural infrastructure and public utility conditions using Tableau dashboards and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA). The study evaluates multiple sectors including energy access, water supply, sanitation, digital connectivity, electricity distribution, and waste management.

Using survey-based infrastructure data, interactive dashboards were developed to identify inequalities, infrastructure gaps, and opportunities for sustainable development.

---

## Objectives

* Assess infrastructure accessibility across multiple sectors.
* Validate hypotheses using visual analytics.
* Identify regional disparities and underserved communities.
* Support evidence-based policy recommendations.
* Demonstrate the power of data visualization in decision-making.

## Key Findings

### Energy Infrastructure

* Low adoption of solar lighting technologies.
* Unequal electricity distribution across regions.
* Dependence on both government and non-government electricity providers.

### Water Infrastructure

* Heavy reliance on groundwater sources.
* Limited formal piped water infrastructure.
* Potential risks of groundwater depletion and contamination.

### Digital Connectivity

* Strong mobile phone penetration.
* Widespread 4G/LTE access.
* Connectivity disparities in geographically challenging areas.

### Sanitation

* Significant use of non-latrine sanitation methods.
* Limited access to private and public sanitation facilities.
* Increased public health risks in underserved communities.

### Waste Management

* Predominant use of drainage ditches and open-ground disposal.
* Environmental contamination risks.
* Need for sustainable wastewater treatment systems.
